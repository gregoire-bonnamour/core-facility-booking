Terms of Use – Cytometry & Microscopy Core Facility

1) Scientific publications

  • Acknowledgment is required for any work supported by the facility.
  • Keep facility staff informed of publications (ideally before submission; and upon acceptance).
  • Mention specific grant numbers if applicable.
  • Co-authorship should be discussed and agreed upon with the facility team when support goes beyond routine.

2) Proper use and care of equipment

  • Mandatory training; access based on competency.
  • Use approved consumables only.
  • Clean and restore the setup at the end of the session.
  • Report incidents immediately via “Report a problem”.

3) Bookings, delays, assistance

  • Modify/Cancel within 30 minutes after start; after that, full charge applies.
  • Assistance subject to staff availability.

4) Data, security, confidentiality

  • Export data to institutional storage at session end.
  • Comply with YourUniversity policies (security, confidentiality, ethics).
  • Institutional email address: You must provide an institutional or professional e‑mail address (e.g., admin@youruniversity.ca). It will be used only for reservation‑related notifications.
  • Contactability: By accepting the regulation, you consent to being contacted at this address by platform staff and other users.

5) Graduated sanctions

  1. Written reminder
  2. Warning and/or invoicing
  3. Temporary suspension of access
  4. Access removal and notification to lab management

6) Contacts

  • Support/incidents: “Report a problem” button (home).
  • Manager: Author Author — admin@youruniversity.ca
  • YourFacility: Official website
