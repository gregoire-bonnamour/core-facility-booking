Terms of Use – Cytometry & Microscopy Core Facility

  • Any scientific publication arising from data acquired on the core facility or from support provided by the facility must include an acknowledgment.
  • The facility staff must be kept informed of publications related to work performed at the facility (ideally before submission to validate the acknowledgment and, if needed, upon acceptance for our records).
  • If specific grants/funding numbers support the instruments, they must be mentioned (the facility can provide the proper codes).
  • If support goes beyond routine (experimental design, acquisition/expert settings, analysis/interpretation, writing), co-authorship should be discussed and agreed upon with the facility team before submission.
